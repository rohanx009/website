‚ôî How to Play ‚ôö

Basic Rules:

  • Click on a piece to select it
  • Click on a valid square to move the piece
  • White pieces are shown in blue, black pieces in red
  • Valid moves will be highlighted when you select a piece

Piece Movements:

  • ‚ôô Pawn: Moves forward 1 square (2 on first move), captures diagonally
  • ‚ôò Knight: Moves in L-shape (2+1 squares)
  • ‚ôó Bishop: Moves diagonally any distance
  • ‚ôñ Rook: Moves horizontally/vertically any distance
  • ‚ôï Queen: Moves in any direction any distance
  • ‚ôî King: Moves one square in any direction

Special Rules:

  • Castling: King moves 2 squares towards rook, rook jumps over (neither piece has moved, path is clear, king not in check)
  • En Passant: Pawn can capture adjacent opponent pawn that just moved 2 squares
  • Pawn Promotion: When pawn reaches opposite end, it becomes Queen, Rook, Bishop, or Knight
  • Check: King is under attack and must move to safety
  • Checkmate: King is in check with no legal moves - game over!
  • Stalemate: No legal moves but not in check - game is a draw

Controls:

  • New Game: Start a fresh game
  • Undo Move: Take back the last move
  • ü§ñ AI Hint: Get an unbiased move suggestion for the current player
  • Help: Show this help dialog

AI Assistant:

  • The AI analyzes the board and suggests the best move
  • Works for both White and Black players without bias
  • Considers piece values, position, threats, and opportunities
  • Highlighted squares show the suggested move (glowing blue)
